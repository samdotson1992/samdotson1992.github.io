What is GIS?


What is GIS? While there is a long a technicial defition you can look up, I usually cater my explanation to each individual. For the insurance agent, I may talk about FEMA flood maps. For the policeman, I would talk crime analysis. But what do I think of GIS? I really can't narrow it down to one thing. Can you make beautiful maps? Yes. Can you use cutting edge technology? Yes. Can you study other planets, power grids, endangered species, climate change, archeology, viruses and political elections? Yes.
